Membership

You can become a member by filling the Registration Form below or by Downloading a copy of the paper Registration Form here

Membership is open to any member of the Nigerian Bar Association upon satisfactory completion of this form and the payment of the Section dues which are:

Section Fees

=N5, 000 (Five Thousand Naira only) per annum for lawyers between 1-7 years

= N10, 000 (Ten Thousand Naira only) per annum for lawyers above 7 years

Please note that payment of ”a.” above entitles you to one free membership to any of the Section’s Committee of your choice, and also note that you can join as many Committees as you can be committed to with an extra N5, 000 for each committee.

The Section is currently made up of fourteen Committees that promote the participation of members of the Association towards achieving the objectives of the Section. Please tick the box beside each of the Committees that you wish to join as listed below and indicate your preferred free membership committee.

METHOD OF PAYMENT
