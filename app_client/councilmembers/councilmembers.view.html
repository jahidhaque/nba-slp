Council Members

The members of the Nigerian Bar Association Section on Legal Practice are drawn from diverse backgrounds and bring their wealth of experience to bear in promoting the objectives of the association.



The Nigerian Bar Association (NBA), in compliance with the provisions of Article IX (b) of the Uniform Bye-Laws of Sections, Institutes and Fora, has setup Steering Committees to administer the affairs of its Section on Legal Practice [SLP] for a maximum period of six (6) weeks and to take steps to organise elections for a new leadership of these Sections and Forum.
